Our History

Kochi Metro Rail Limited (KMRL) was established in 2012 to implement and operate the Kochi Metro project. The project was envisioned to provide a modern, efficient, and sustainable public transportation system for the city of Kochi, addressing its growing traffic congestion and enhancing urban mobility. The first phase of the metro line, spanning from Aluva to Palarivattom, was inaugurated in 2017, marking a significant milestone in the city's infrastructure development.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to deliver a world-class metro system that is safe, reliable, and environmentally friendly, contributing to the socio-economic development of Kochi. Our vision is to be a leader in urban transportation, continuously innovating and expanding our services to meet the evolving needs of the community.
